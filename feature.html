Company Features

At Rich Group, we bring together the best people to do the work, structuring our teams to enhance interaction with clients. We blend efficient project management capabilities with a commitment to client service and rigorous quantitative analysis. Because we believe that excellent ideas benefit our clients no matter where they come from thewithin our organization, we often collaborate across offices, and we encourage and recognize team members at levels who voice their opinion and contribute. The people who thrive at Analysis Group have an opportunity to contribute in ways that are intellectually stimulating and professionally rewarding, because their work results in a better outcome for our clients.


Our close ties with leading experts from academia, industry, and government ensure that clients have access to the people and ideas that will make a difference in addressing their issues. The rigor they bring to bear means that our work, whether under the direction of our affiliated or internal experts, will meet the most exacting standards of quality in the courtroom, the boardroom, or the media.
